Notification Panel — Hover-to-read

Selected design: hover an unread notification for ~700ms to mark it as read. A progress bar fills from top to bottom while hovering. Clicking the item navigates. "Mark all as read" button available. Two bg variants shown side by side.

Variant 1 — Standard panel bg
Uses --color-bg-secondary as the panel background. Consistent with existing dropdowns and the user menu.
