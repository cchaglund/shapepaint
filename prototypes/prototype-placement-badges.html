A. Corner Ribbon

Classic • Bold

A folded diagonal ribbon wrapping the top-right corner of the card. Reads immediately as "award" without needing an icon. The angled text adds energy to the composition.

B. Bottom Banner

Graphic • Retro

A full-width colored bar across the bottom of the artwork, like a sports scoreboard or awards ceremony banner. The top of the art is completely unobscured. The banner doubles as a visual "podium" — gold is prominent, silver recedes, bronze is warm and earthy.
